Sno' More

Enjoy the snow without the hassle of dealing with the snow. Whether you are unable to shovel or you just looked outside your window and thought to yourself: "I really cant't deal with this right now...". We have your solution!

Our application Sno' More is designed so you can post a shoveling request, and a shoveler will come and shovel your snow away! We will send you text notifications to keep you updated throughout the process.
